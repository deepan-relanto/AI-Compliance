The workflow

From job description to hire decision — fully automated

R-Conduct AI handles every step. Your recruiters only see the shortlist and evidence package.

1
Upload your JD and candidate pool
R-Conduct AI reads your job description and every CV. It maps competencies, spots gaps, and generates a unique question set for each candidate — before the first interview starts.
2
AI agent joins the meeting
The agent joins Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, or Webex as a participant. It verifies the candidate's identity against their application and begins the proctored session.
3
Adaptive interview with full signal capture
Questions adapt in real time based on responses. Voice tone, confidence, hesitation, and facial cues are analyzed simultaneously. Browser behavior and keystrokes are monitored throughout.
4
Evidence-backed recommendation delivered
You receive a scored report: competency breakdown, behavioral flags, transcript highlights, and a clear hire or pass recommendation — with the evidence to back every call.
